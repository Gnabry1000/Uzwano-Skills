Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Uzwano Skills is committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring your data is used responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, surname, and UJ email for account creation.
  • Profile and skill details you share publicly.
  • Session and payment information when booking or teaching.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To connect learners and tutors safely.
  • To process payments and improve platform quality.
  • To ensure compliance with university and legal policies.

3. Your Rights

You can update or delete your account at any time by contacting us at support@uzwano.co.za.
