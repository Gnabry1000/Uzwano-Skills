Pricing & Fees

Uzwano Skills believes in **fair pricing for students** — affordable for learners and rewarding for tutors.

  • Minimum rate: R10/hour
  • Maximum rate: R100/hour
  • Uzwano platform fee: 15% per transaction
  • Tutors receive payment instantly after sessions are confirmed.

Example:

If you charge R80/hr, you’ll receive R68/hr after the 15% platform fee.

The platform fee helps us maintain security, host services, and improve features for the UJ learning community.
