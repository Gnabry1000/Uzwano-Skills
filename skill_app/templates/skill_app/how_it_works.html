How It Works

Uzwano Skills connects students who want to **learn** with those who want to **teach** — all within the UJ community.

  1. Sign Up: Create your account using your UJ email and set up your skill interests.
  2. Explore Skills: Browse a variety of skills offered by other students — from coding to photography.
  3. Book a Session: Pick a tutor, agree on a time, and start learning.
  4. Teach & Earn: Offer your own skills, set your hourly rate (R10–R100), and earn money — Uzwano only takes 15%.

Whether you’re learning or teaching, Uzwano makes skill-sharing simple, safe, and affordable.
