Tutor Guide

Teaching on Uzwano is your chance to share what you know and earn while helping others grow.

1. Build Your Profile

Make sure your name, picture, and bio are filled out — learners trust tutors with complete profiles.

2. Create a Skill Ad

List what you teach, describe your approach, and set your rate (R10–R100/hr). Be clear and inviting!

3. Manage Your Sessions

Respond to learners quickly, agree on free days, and keep communication professional and friendly.

4. Get Paid

After the session is complete and verified, you’ll receive your earnings minus the 15% fee.

Remember: great tutors focus on clarity, patience, and engagement — not just knowledge.
